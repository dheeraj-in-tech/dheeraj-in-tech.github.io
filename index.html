What I actually do

I currently work as Senior Support Manager at Cognizant Technologies, running 24×7 production and application support for Cloud SaaS products for a US client, with a team of 50 spread across onshore and offshore locations. Over two decades I've moved from hands-on troubleshooting to building the support function itself — standing up technical centers, writing the incident and change playbooks, negotiating SLAs, and hiring and mentoring the engineers who run them. My technical grounding (AWS, data pipelines, Python, SQL, and — more recently — LLMs and agentic AI, backed by an M.Tech in AI & Data Science) means I can sit in the same room as engineering leadership and hold my own, while still speaking the customer's language.
